Questions

Not a personality test.
A project tool.

Is this just another personality test?+
No. PreForma is not a psychological assessment. It analyzes collaboration patterns, communication styles, and decision-making behaviors that directly affect how a project runs — how many revisions a client tends to request, how quickly they make decisions, how much oversight they need. The output is operational, not clinical.
Does my client know they're being analyzed?+
The questionnaire is framed as a standard project intake — questions about how your client approaches decisions, what matters most to them in a project, and how they prefer to communicate. There is no deception involved, and the questions are entirely appropriate for any professional onboarding process.
How long does the questionnaire take?+
3 to 4 minutes. It's designed to feel effortless for your client — no psychological jargon, no complex scales. Just straightforward questions about their project preferences and working style.
What do I actually receive?+
Five PDF documents delivered to your inbox automatically: an Executive Summary with key insights, a Risk-Adjusted Contract with clauses built specifically around this client's risk profile, an Adaptive Project Scope tailored to the project type, a Client Communication Strategy with phase-by-phase guidance on how to lead this client, and a Client Dynamics Profile with a full behavioral risk map — including three things you should never do with this client, and a conflict resolution protocol if something goes wrong.
How accurate is it?+
The system includes a confidence indicator based on how clearly defined the client's profile is. When responses point strongly in one direction, confidence is high. When patterns are more mixed, the report flags this explicitly and recommends verifying assumptions in conversation. PreForma is a decision-support tool — it gives you a significant head start, not a guarantee.
How does the subscription work?+
Choose The Blueprint ($39/month, 3 analyses) or The Foundation ($79/month, 10 analyses). Credits reset at the start of each billing period. Cancel anytime — no lock-in. Your first report is always free so you can evaluate the system before committing.
Can I use it for different project types?+
Yes. PreForma adapts to 10 project types — from residential interiors to hospitality, commercial spaces, design & build, and luxury projects. The scope document, risk assessment, and contract clauses all adjust based on the project type and scale your client selects during the questionnaire.
