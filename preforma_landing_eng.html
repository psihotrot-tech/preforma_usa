Process

How it works.

Your answers help your designer understand your decision-making style and approach to a project — before the first meeting. No psychological jargon. No right or wrong answers.

01
Complete the questionnaire
Ten short questions about your needs and approach to the project. It takes about 3 minutes and reads like a standard project intake.
02
Analysis in the background
Your responses are processed through a system developed specifically for the design and architecture context — identifying collaboration and communication patterns relevant to the project.
03
A better project
Your designer enters the first meeting already understanding your approach — less guesswork, clearer communication, and a process built around how you actually work.
🔒
Your responses are private
The results are shared exclusively with your designer as a preparation tool. You will not receive a report or a score — the questionnaire exists to improve collaboration, not to evaluate you as a person.
