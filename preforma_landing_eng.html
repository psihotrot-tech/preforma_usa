How it works ↓
The process

Three steps.
No surprises.

Your answers help your designer understand your decision-making style and approach to the project — before the first meeting. No psychological jargon. No evaluation.

STEP 01
Answer 10 short statements
Each statement is about how you prefer to approach decisions, communication, and your project. It reads like a standard project intake — takes about 3 minutes.
STEP 02
Your responses are analyzed
The system maps your answers to a collaboration and communication profile relevant to your specific project type. No labels, no diagnoses — just patterns.
STEP 03
Your designer prepares
Your designer receives a preparation report that helps them communicate with you more effectively — from the first meeting through project handover.
🔒
Your responses are private
The results are shared exclusively with your designer as a preparation tool. You will not receive a report or a score — the questionnaire exists to improve collaboration, not to evaluate you as a person. Your answers are never shared with third parties.
